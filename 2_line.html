Gender Imbalance in Western Asia

This trend is attributed to the higher demand for male migrant labor in these countries, outpacing the demand for female migrant labor. Temporary labor migration contracts are prevalent in the region, leading to a situation where female family members rarely accompany male migrants.

In most regions, the number of male and female migrants in 2020 was roughly equivalent, where the share of women and girls among all migrants was slightly less than 50 per cent.
